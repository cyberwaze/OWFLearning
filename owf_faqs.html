Open Questions

Here is a list of open questions that through research we could not confirm regarding the implementation of OWFs.

1.) Are the same communication protocols being used in offshore wind farms as onshore wind farms?

2.) Are the control systems on the onshore wind farms the same as offshore wind farms?

3.) Is there an assurance plan if a cybersecurity threat does occur?

4.) What policies and regulations need to be set to protect the onshore and offshore wind farms from cybersecurity threats?

5.) Have there been any verified hacks of offshore wind farms?

6.) Specific turbines being used in US offshore wind farms include GE’s Haliade-X and Siemens Gamesa SG. Have cybersecurity vulnerabilities potentially present in these turbines and associated control firmware/hardware been scrutinized?

7.) Do OWFs emit or receive any kind of navigational information such as AIS?

8.) Has a reference architecture for US OWFs been established?

9.) Are test beds or “digital twins” being established to perform ongoing cybersecurity penetration testing of OWFs?
